Align Left Example

Mountains are large natural elevations of the earth surface. This paragraph wraps around the image because the image uses align="left". The text flows on the right side of the image. This demonstrates old-style HTML image alignment.









Align Right Example

Modern cities contain tall buildings, roads, vehicles, and transportation systems. This text wraps around the image because the image uses align="right". The paragraph appears on the left side of the image.
